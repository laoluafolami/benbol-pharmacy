## How to Add Signature in Hostinger Webmail

### Step 1: Access Settings
1. Log in to Hostinger webmail
2. Click the gear icon (⚙️) in the top right
3. Select "Settings" from the dropdown

### Step 2: Navigate to Identities
1. In the left sidebar, click "Identities"
2. Click on your email address (info@benbolpharmacy.com)

### Step 3: Add Signature
1. Scroll down to the "Signature" section
2. Check the box "HTML signature"
3. Paste the HTML signature code (Option 1, 2, or 3)
4. Check "Automatically add signature to new messages"
5. Check "Automatically add signature to replies"
6. Click "Save" at the bottom

### Step 4: Test
1. Compose a new email
2. Verify the signature appears at the bottom
3. Send a test email to yourself to see how it looks

## Professional Email Signature Best Practices

### Do's ✅
- Keep it simple and professional
- Include essential contact information
- Use consistent branding (colors, fonts)
- Make phone numbers and emails clickable
- Include a brief tagline or value proposition
- Add a confidentiality notice for healthcare
- Test on multiple email clients

### Don'ts ❌
- Don't use too many colors or fonts
- Don't make it too long (keep under 6 lines)
- Don't use large images (slows loading)
- Don't include social media unless active
- Don't use animated GIFs (unprofessional)
- Don't forget mobile responsiveness

## Troubleshooting

### Signature Not Appearing
- Ensure "HTML signature" is checked
- Verify "Automatically add signature" is enabled
- Clear browser cache and reload
- Try logging out and back in

### Formatting Issues
- Use tables for better email client compatibility
- Avoid CSS that's not inline
- Test in multiple email clients (Gmail, Outlook, etc.)
- Keep images hosted on reliable servers

### Links Not Working
- Ensure `href` attributes are properly formatted
- Use `tel:` for phone numbers
- Use `mailto:` for email addresses
- Use full URLs with `https://` for websites
