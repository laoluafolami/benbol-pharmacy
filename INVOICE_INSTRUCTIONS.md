   - Invoice number

   - Notes and payment terms

2. **Automatic Calculations**

   - Subtotal

3. **Actions**
   - Preview invoice before downloading

   - Edit all details

   - Clean layout
   - Itemized breakdown
   - Payment terms section

## Notes

The invoice includes payment terms suggesting 50% upfront and 50% upon completion. You can adjust these terms in the "Notes" section before generating the final PDF.

Bank details placeholder is included - update with actual banking information before sending to the client.

## Customization

You can:
- Modify any line item
- Add new services
- Remove services
- Adjust rates
- Change quantities
- Update client information
- Modify payment terms

All changes are reflected in real-time in the totals section.
